About me

My name is Victoria, I love art, music, history. I study design because I consider myself a creative person and I like to draw. Below is a list of 5 facts about me:

  • My favorite book is The Cursed Kings by Maurice Druon
  • My favorite music group is AC DC
  • My favorite movie is Harry Potter
  • My favorite quote Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts. Winston Churchill
  • I like sour marmalade

I have an unusual hobby, I like to study genealogy and the history of my family. When I have free time, I read archival materials and study the history of their lives. So far I have been able to find my ancestors before the 17 th century. In my opinion, history is much more interesting to read when you know where your ancestors were at that time.
