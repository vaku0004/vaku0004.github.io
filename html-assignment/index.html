Introduction

My name is Viktoria. I like art, music, history. I am a creative person and I like painting.I chose the Interactive Media Design program because it seems to me interesting and in demand in the future. I am interested in studying illustration, web design, motion design and video production. I hope that this knowledge will help me in the future.

Also, I have an unusual hobby, I like to study genealogy and the history of my family. When I have free time, I read archival materials on the website of the CHAM. So far I have been able to find my ancestors before the 17th century. In my opinion, history is much more interesting to read when you know where your ancestors were at that time.
