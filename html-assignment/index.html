About me

My name is Viktoria. I like art, music, history and nature. Below is a list of 5 facts about me:

  • My favorite book is The Accursed Kings by Maurice Druon
  • My favorite music band is ACDC
  • My favorite movie is Harry Potter
  • My favorite quote is Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts. Winston Churchill
  • My favorite subject at school was math

I study design because I am a creative person and I like painting. Also, I have an unusual hobby, I like to study genealogy and the history of my family. When I have free time, I read archival materials on the website of the CHAM. So far I have been able to find my ancestors before the 17th century. In my opinion, history is much more interesting to read when you know where your ancestors were at that time.

I would prioritize my hobbies in the following order:
  1. Music
  2. Illustration
  3. Genealogy
  4. History
